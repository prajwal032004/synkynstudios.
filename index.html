Technology

How It Works

We follow a systematic process to deliver high-quality product.

STEP 1

Separate Humidity from Cooling

Traditional HVAC forces your compressor and coils to cool humid air, wasting massive energy overcooling air just to fight moisture.

Synkyn Studios removes humidity first. Dry air flows to your cooling system. Your AC finally does the one job it was designed to do, instead of two.

STEP 2

The Synkyn MICRA Filter

Our patented filter is unlike anything commercial HVAC has ever seen.

What makes it different:
Synkyn MICRA filter is a rigid material embedded with helical channels. Think of it as a precision-engineered sponge that spins liquid droplets out of airflow and captures them instantly, with no moving parts.

  • Liquid desiccant sprays into incoming humid air
  • Desiccant absorbs moisture on contact
  • Synkyn MICRA filter separates desiccant droplets with moisture from dry air with minimal pressure drop
  • Unique design virtually eliminates carryover

STEP 3

Closed-Loop Regeneration

  • Our regenerator removes the water from the desiccant
  • Dry, cooled air flows to your HVAC system
  • Warm, humid air vents to the outdoors

The result:

  • Compact, lightweight systems that fit where others can’t
  • No drains. No plumbing.
  • Continuous operation with minimal maintenance
