Our Story

Synkyn Studio Story

Dhiraj Kishore, our CEO, founded Synkyn Studios with a bold vision — to build a cinematic, AI-first creative studio right from Bengaluru, India.

The challenge was clear: brands needed premium visuals fast, but traditional production was slow, expensive, and hard to scale. There had to be a better way.

That's where Synkyn Studios was born — directing generative image and video with a filmmaker's eye, powered by AI.

Today, we help brands create immersive visual experiences — from advertising campaigns to full brand identities — at speeds that were never possible before.
